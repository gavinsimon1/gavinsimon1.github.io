SERVICES

I offer a comprehensive suite of digital marketing services designed to help your business achieve its online goals. By combining strategic planning with hands-on execution, I ensure your digital presence is not only visible but also drives measurable growth.

Digital Marketing Strategy & Consultation

Every successful digital journey begins with a clear roadmap. I work closely with you to understand your business objectives, target audience, and competitive landscape. This allows me to develop bespoke digital marketing strategies that align with your vision and deliver tangible results. From market research and competitor analysis to identifying key performance indicators (KPIs), I provide the strategic guidance you need to make informed decisions and maximize your return on investment.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Being found online is crucial. My SEO services are focused on improving your organic search rankings, driving more qualified traffic to your website. This includes in-depth keyword research, on-page optimization (website content, meta descriptions, headings), technical SEO audits (site speed, mobile-friendliness), and off-page strategies (link building). The goal is to enhance your website's authority and visibility, ensuring potential customers can easily discover your products or services.

Paid Advertising (PPC) Management

Accelerate your growth with targeted paid advertising campaigns. I specialize in Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising across various platforms, including Google Ads and social media advertising. This involves creating compelling ad copy, optimizing bidding strategies, audience targeting, and continuous campaign monitoring to ensure maximum efficiency and ROI. Whether your goal is lead generation, increased sales, or brand awareness, I'll craft and manage campaigns that deliver on your objectives.

Website Development & Optimization (WordPress)

Your website is your digital storefront. I leverage my WordPress expertise to develop user-friendly, responsive, and search-engine-optimized websites. This includes everything from initial site setup and design to content integration and ongoing maintenance. If you already have a WordPress site, I can provide optimization services to improve its performance, user experience, and conversion rates, ensuring it effectively supports your marketing efforts. I also work with Shopify, SquareSpace and Wix websites.

Data Analysis & Performance Reporting

What gets measured gets improved. I provide transparent and insightful data analysis and performance reporting for all campaigns. By tracking key metrics, I identify trends, uncover opportunities, and make data-driven adjustments to continually optimize your digital marketing efforts. You'll receive regular, easy-to-understand reports that clearly illustrate the impact of my work on your business's bottom line.
